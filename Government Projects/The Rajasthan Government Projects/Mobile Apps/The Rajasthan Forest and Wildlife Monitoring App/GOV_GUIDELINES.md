# Government Compliance & Guidelines

This application is engineered to align with the legislative framework of the **Forest Department, Government of Rajasthan** and **Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC)**.

## 1. Compliance with The Wildlife (Protection) Act, 1972

| App Feature                       | Relevant Section     | Compliance Mechanism                                                                                               |
| :-------------------------------- | :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Drishti AI (Object Detection)** | **Sec 2(16), 9, 39** | Automatically categorizes species into Schedule I-IV. Ensures `hunting` is flagged immediately with GPS stamps.    |
| **Apradh Panjiyan**               | **Sec 50**           | Supports the "Power of Entry, Search, Arrest and Detention" by providing digital forms for Seizure Memos (`Fard`). |
| **Vajra-Kavach**                  | **Sec 55(c)**        | Ensures that electronic evidence gathered serves as cognizable evidence in courts by maintaining hash integrity.   |

## 2. Compliance with The Rajasthan Forest Act, 1953

| App Feature             | Relevant Section | Compliance Mechanism                                                                       |
| :---------------------- | :--------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Gasti Path (Patrol)** | **Sec 64**       | Logs "beat duties" to prove official presence and action taken to prevent forest offenses. |
| **Offense Logging**     | **Sec 33, 34**   | Digital reporting of illicit felling, quarrying, or encroachment in Protected Forests.     |

## 3. Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP)

- **Data Fiduciary:** Department of Forest, Government of Rajasthan.
- **Purpose Limitation:** Data collected is strictly for _law enforcement and monitoring_.
- **Storage Limitation:** Local data ("Laghu-Bhandar") is auto-purged after successful sync to the secure 10T Grid.

## 4. Rajasthan Transparency in Public Procurement (RTPP) Act

- The transparent "NoC Tracker" allows public interface (via DFO Dashboard) to see the status of their applications, ensuring accountability and reducing delays in developmental projects.
