ChitraHarsha VPK Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially the farmers of Rajasthan. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act** and **Information Technology Act, 2000**.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Identity Information**: Jan-Aadhaar details, name, and district (collected via secure government SSO).
- **Agricultural Data**: Crop listings, land location, and organic certifications.
- **Biometric/Digital Identity**: Digital signatures and voice recordings (processed via Krishi Vaani AI).
- **Technical Data**: Device ID, IP address, and sync status for MeghKosh synchronization.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To facilitate e-mandi listings and transactions.
- To verify identity via government databases.
- To provide AI-driven voice assistance for rural accessibility.
- To ensure compliance with Rajasthan Government agricultural policies.

## 3. Data Protection & Security

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted using **AES-256-GCM** hardware-accelerated protocols.
- **Data Sharding**: We utilize a 10-trillion record-ready sharding architecture to ensure no single point of failure.
- **Offline First**: Data is stored locally in an encrypted format (MeghKosh) before syncing with state servers.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with:

- **Government of Rajasthan** departments for scheme disbursement and verification.
- **OpenForge** for secure software updates.
- Financial institutions for direct benefit transfers (DBT).

## 5. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

- Access and correct your data.
- Withdraw consent for processing.
- Request erasure of your data (subject to government record-keeping laws).
