# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 25, 2025**

This Privacy Policy outlines how the **Rajasthan Forest & Wildlife Monitoring System** collects, uses, and protects information. We are committed to ensuring your privacy strictly in accordance with the **Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW)** and the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**.

## 1. Information Collection

- **Personal Information**: We do not automatically capture any specific personal information (like name, phone number, or e-mail address) that allows us to identify you individually unless you voluntarily choose to provide such information (e.g., sending an SOS alert or filing an offense report).
- **System Logs**: We record your visit and log the following information for statistical and security purposes:
  - IP address and server address.
  - Date and time of access.
  - Pages accessed and documents downloaded.
  - Browser type and Operating System.

## 2. Use of Information

- **Official Purpose**: Any personal data collected (e.g., Officer ID, Beat Location) is used exclusively for the administration of forest duties, patrol monitoring, and emergency response.
- **No Commercial Sharing**: We do not sell or share any personally identifiable information volunteered on this site to any third party (public/private). Any information provided to this application will be protected from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

## 3. Data Retention

- Data related to forest offenses (Apradh Panjiyan) is retained indefinitely as per the **Rajasthan Public Records Act**.
- Temporary patrol logs are archived every fiscal year.

## 4. Cookies

- This application uses local storage ("Laghu-Bhandar") to improve performance and enable offline functionality. It does not use persistent tracking cookies to monitor your surfing habits outside this application.

## 5. Security (Vajra-Kavach)

- We implement high-security measures including **AES-256 Encryption** to protect data during transit and rest.
- Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

## 6. Changes to this Policy

- The Government of Rajasthan / System Administrators reserve the right to change this policy at any time. Updates will be timely notified on the dashboard.
