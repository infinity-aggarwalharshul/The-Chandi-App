# Raj Kissan Organic X - Detailed Project Report (DPR)

**Project Name:** The ChitraHarsha Raj Kissan Organic X
**Department:** Department of Agriculture, Government of Rajasthan
**Developer:** The ChitraHarsha VPK Ventures
**Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## 1. Project Overview

**Raj Kissan Organic X** is a specialized version of the "World's Most Powerful" monitoring architecture, tailored for the agricultural sector. It aims to digitize the **Organic Certification Process** and monitor crop health using offline-first AI ("Krishi-Drishti").

### Vision

To empower every "Kissan" (Farmer) in Rajasthan with a scientific, AI-driven assistant that works without internet connectivity in remote fields.
