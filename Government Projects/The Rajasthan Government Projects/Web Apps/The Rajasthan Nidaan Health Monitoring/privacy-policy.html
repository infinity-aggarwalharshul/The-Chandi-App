Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

The ChitraHarsha VPK Ventures operates the Raj Kissan Organic X application for the Department of Agriculture, Government of Rajasthan.

1. Information We Collect

  • Farmer Identity: Name, Kissan ID, Jan Aadhaar (for subsidy verification).
  • Land Data: GPS coordinates of farm plots (Geo-tagging for Organic Certification).
  • Crop Data: Photos of crops, pest issues, and soil health status.
  • Audio: Voice notes regarding harvest details or grievances.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Issuing Organic Farming Certificates (PKVY / NPOP Standards).
  • AI-based Pest & Disease diagnosis (Krishi-Drishti).
  • Disbursing government subsidies directly to bank accounts (DBT).

3. Data Protection

We use Vajra-Kavach (AES-256-GCM) encryption. All farm data stays within India (Meghraj Cloud Policy). We do not share farmer data with private corporations without consent.

4. Your Rights

Under the Rajasthan Transparency in Public Procurement (RTPP) Act and DPDP Act 2023, farmers have the right to access and correct their land records stored in this app.
