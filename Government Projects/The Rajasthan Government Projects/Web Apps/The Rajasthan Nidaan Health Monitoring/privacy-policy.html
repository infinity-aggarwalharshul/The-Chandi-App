Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2024

The ChitraHarsha VPK Ventures ("we," "our," or "us") operates The ChitraHarsha Nidaan 2.0 AI Health Monitoring System ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal and health information when you use our Application.

Important: By using this Application, you consent to the data practices described in this policy in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and other applicable Indian laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Identification Information

  • ABHA ID / Jan Aadhaar Number: For unique patient identification
  • SSO ID: For healthcare worker authentication
  • Name and Contact Details: As voluntarily provided

1.2 Health Information

  • Symptoms and Medical Conditions: Text and voice input data
  • Patient Images: Photos of symptoms, lab reports, or patient condition
  • Audio Recordings: Consultation recordings (with explicit consent)
  • Diagnostic Data: AI-generated health predictions and analytics

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Location Data: Geographic coordinates for field-level health mapping (encrypted)
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent, interaction patterns
  • IP Address: For security and audit purposes (hashed)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Healthcare Delivery: To provide medical diagnosis support and health monitoring
  • Data Analytics: To predict disease outbreaks and health trends
  • Record Keeping: To maintain electronic health records (EHR)
  • Communication: To send health alerts and notifications

2.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Research & Development: Anonymized data for medical research (with consent)
  • System Improvement: To enhance AI algorithms and user experience
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Data Protection & Encryption

3.1 Encryption Standards

All sensitive personal data is encrypted using:

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard)
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Transport Security: TLS 1.3 for all data transmission

3.2 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Google Firebase (ISO 27001 certified)
  • Backup: Rajasthan State Data Centre (SDC) - simulated via Firestore
  • Local Storage: Encrypted IndexedDB for offline capabilities

3.3 Access Control

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all users
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Audit logging of all data access and modifications

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 Authorized Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Authorized doctors and medical staff
  • Government Authorities: Department of Health, Rajasthan Government
  • Emergency Services: In case of medical emergencies
  • Research Institutions: Anonymized data only, with explicit consent

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud hosting and database
  • Compliance: All third parties are bound by strict data protection agreements

4.3 We Will NOT

  • Sell your personal or health data to any third party
  • Use your data for marketing without explicit consent
  • Share identifiable data for non-medical purposes

5. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023 Compliance)

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

5.2 Right to Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

5.3 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

5.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@chitraharshavpk.in

6. Data Retention

  • Active Health Records: 3 years from last activity
  • Audio Recordings: 30 days (unless flagged for medical importance)
  • Images: 1 year (medical necessity)
  • Audit Logs: 7 years (compliance requirement)
  • Anonymized Research Data: Indefinitely

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management and authentication
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (opt-in)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies

8. Children's Privacy

This Application is intended for use by healthcare professionals. Patient data for minors (under 18) should only be entered by authorized guardians or healthcare providers with proper consent documentation.

9. International Data Transfer

Your data is primarily stored in India. If transferred internationally (e.g., Firebase servers), we ensure:

  • Adequate data protection measures equivalent to Indian standards
  • Compliance with cross-border data transfer regulations
  • Encryption during transit

10. Security Measures

  • Encryption: AES-256-GCM for data at rest and in transit
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Monitoring: 24/7 intrusion detection
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups
  • Incident Response: Dedicated security team

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

  • Affected users will be notified within 72 hours
  • Regulatory authorities will be informed as required
  • Corrective measures will be implemented immediately

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with updated "Effective Date"
  • Notified to users via in-app notification
  • Subject to your continued consent for material changes
